About VoltStation

VoltStation is a community-driven EV charging management platform that enables neighbors, workplaces, and communities to share solar energy and charge affordably. Our tools help match charging demand with local solar generation to reduce costs and carbon emissions.

What is Community Charging?

Community Charging connects local EV drivers with shared charging infrastructure, optimizing charging times to make the most of available solar generation and reduced grid costs.

Our Mission

To enable affordable, renewable-forward EV charging at the community level through fair pricing, smart scheduling and simple tools.

Our Vision

A world where residents and businesses share clean energy to power electric mobility, lowering costs and emissions for everyone.

Benefits of Community EV Charging

  • Lower charging costs
  • Better use of local solar
  • Reduced grid strain
  • Community engagement

Smart Energy Sharing

Our platform schedules charging during solar-rich windows, and prices adapt to station occupancy so early adopters save more.

Renewable Energy Integration

We integrate solar forecasts and building load to identify surplus windows for cheaper charging.

Affordable Charging

Dynamic pricing and community sharing help lower per-kWh costs compared to unmanaged charging.

Sustainable Transportation

By prioritizing solar, we reduce the carbon intensity of every kWh used for charging.
