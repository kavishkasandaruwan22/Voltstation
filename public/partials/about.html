Who we are

VoltStation is a community-driven EV charging platform that connects EV owners with nearby charging stations through smart booking, real-time availability, and renewable-energy-aware pricing.

Our mission

  • Promote sustainable transportation
  • Support renewable energy usage
  • Grow community charging access
  • Make charging management efficient and transparent

Our vision

To create Sri Lanka’s largest smart community EV charging network built around convenience, sustainability, and reliable service.

Features

Smart Booking

Reserve chargers with a guided booking experience.

Live Availability

See station and bay status at a glance.

Renewable Energy

Charge around solar surplus and lower-carbon periods.

Dynamic Pricing

Benefit from solar-aware and time-based rates.

Community Charging

Support shared infrastructure across neighborhoods.

Secure Payments

Manage bookings and payments in one secure place.
