Charging day workflow

  1. Arrive and park in a normal university parking bay.
  2. Wait for VoltStation to publish your appointment.
  3. Move to the assigned AC charging bay when notified.
  4. Charge at your fixed AC power for the assigned continuous slot.
  5. Move out by the turnover deadline so the next user can charge.

What the optimizer chooses

It chooses only the AC charger, charging start time, charging end time, and turnover period. It does not choose a DC charger and it does not change charging speed.
